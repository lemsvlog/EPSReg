EPS-TOPIK Eligibility Criteria

  • Persons aged 18 to 38 (born between Aug. 26, 1986 and Aug. 25, 2007)
  • Persons with no criminal record of imprisonment or heavier punishment
  • Persons with no forced deportation records or deportation orders from Korea
  • Persons with no restrictions (disqualifying factors) on leaving the Philippines
  • Persons with no condition of color blindness or color weakness
  • Those who have suffered a herniated disc, finger amputation, etc., may apply but will be thoroughly assessed in the 2nd round of screening for their job capacity
  • Persons with no existing or active EPS application
  • Persons with no experience in staying in South Korea for five (5) years or longer under non-professional employment (E-9) and seafarer employment (E-10) visa
  • Persons who are not diagnosed with infectious diseases such as syphilis, tuberculosis, hepatitis, etc.

EPS-TOPIK Document Requirements

  • ID Photo (Passport-sized)
    • Taken against a white background within the last 6 months
    • File size: 13 KB or below
    • Format: JPG
    • Dimensions: 300px (w) × 400px (h)
  • Scanned Copy of Passport
    • Without signature
    • No discoloration — only blue color allowed
    • File size: 90 KB or below
    • Format: JPG
    • Dimensions: 800px (w) × 600px (h)
